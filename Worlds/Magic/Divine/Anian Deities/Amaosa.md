The three-fold embodiment of family. Familial love is complex and multi-faceted, changing throughout life. As a child, family is safety and excitement for the future; as an adult, family is to be nurtured and respected; while ancestors look upon their families with compassion and reverence for the past. She embodies all three of these facets at once, taking on the form of whichever matches the observer's intention, mirroring their species and some likeness, but with red violet eyes, if not all three at once.
