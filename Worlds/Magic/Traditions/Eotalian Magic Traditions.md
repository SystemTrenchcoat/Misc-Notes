There are many sources to draw from when it comes to Eotalian Magic, from elements, to planes, to gods, however, most do not engage with magic through these fairly specific avenues. It is more common to focus on magic through how one intends on using it, creating a variety of Magical Traditions. Most will only study individual traditions instead of the theory and sources behind them, but, like with understanding code or computer engineering, understanding the components that make traditions and spells work makes it easier to alter them at the base, if not make an original one.

Below is a list of some known Magical Traditions, but a comprehensive list does not exist at this time.

- Daoshi - Destruction magic
- Rekone -  Healing and reparation magic
- Aurai - Enhancement and augmentation magic
- Seriin - Performance and sensational magic
- Ngingo - Animation and sentience altering magic
- Ashan - Summoning and manifesting magic
- Cyrua - Weapon enhancement
- Briosai - Martial art centered
