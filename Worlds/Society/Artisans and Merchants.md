Artisans, master crafters, excel in the art of creating. While many own shops, most of their businesses come via referral, often by merchant.

Merchants, master networkers and negotiators, excel in the art of people. While few are able to produce wares of their won, they are highly adept at connecting product to people.

Common practice among artisans is to form contracts with merchant guilds to spread their wares and word of them across the world (or continent depending (mostly on time) ). Wares are sold x% below asking price which is made back via x% of the sale. Contrastly, the artisan offers a form of token to the merchants marked with their shop and location and occasionally a bonus (ex. 25% off) of sorts. When presented and used at or below the cut the artisan makes from sales (x%)
