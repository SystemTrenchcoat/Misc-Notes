## Overview

A moderately sized town in the middle of the [[Mageia|ever-changing lands]] famous for its exemplary meade. The constant change of the environment was once a mild problem for the town, but like many civilizations, they adapted. The architects of the town added sigils across town to prevent the land from changing. It is unknown for sure how it was done. Some suggest she petitioned a god while others suggest she drew on the void's power. Regardless of how it was done, it worked and the architect was never seen again. The meade the town is famous for is crafted from special berries that have evolved to adapt to the land around town. The unique flavor has yet to be replicated and it is rumored that [[Aproveleous]] uses the meade in a special drink of his own.
