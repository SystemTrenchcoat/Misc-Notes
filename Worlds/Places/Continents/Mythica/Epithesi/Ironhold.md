## Overview

A bustling steampunk town amidst the blazing canyons within [[Epithesi]]. This technologically advanced town has significant ties to the [[Engineering College]] for its expertise. The people of this town are run by a direct democracy and much of its economy comes from trade of [clockwork](Clockwork%20Engineering) goods. The town requires its youth to go to school for one of various technological trades which they are  expected to work in for much of their careers. On occassion, people come from the [[Engineering College]] in [[Mageia]] which the students are obligated to attend or otherwise participate in.
