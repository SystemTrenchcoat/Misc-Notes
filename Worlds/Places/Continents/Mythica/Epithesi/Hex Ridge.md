## Overview

A town where "law" is more of a suggestion than anything. Fuming with bandits and thieves, this town is far from a safe place to live. If the bandits weren't bad enough, they all have dangerous weapons unlike anything in Mythica: guns enhanced with magic. The sheriffs can't keep up with the advancements of the outlaws, the gunsmiths in town arm both sides of the law, so even if the sheriff wanted to arrest them, they would be shooting themselves in the foot.
