## Overview

They say there's a town at the bottom of the hill, where poor souls go when they die. This is that town. All of the thieves and bandits go here when they die, a special purgatory just for them. There is no law to speak of here, just who wants it more? The outlaws control everything in town and the constant power struggle leaves the place in constant turmoil. No one knows why the damned are called here, but fortunately, there are no villagers to care, only the dead.
