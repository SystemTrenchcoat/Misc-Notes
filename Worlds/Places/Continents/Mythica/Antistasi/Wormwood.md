## Overview

A small village on the outskirts of [Antistasi's Forest](Antistasi) approaching the [[Syngrou Mountains|Syngrou Mountain Range]]. The village is predominantly a farming village, growing hearty vegetation along the mountain ridge. There are, however, a few people who make ornate threads, yarns, and textiles which also sell for a pretty penny. The villagers mostly keep to themselves as they are, by nature, a peaceful people. That said, they are quite skilled at defending themselves if need be.
