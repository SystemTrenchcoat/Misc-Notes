## Overview

A warm, small village in southeast [[Prostasia]] well known for its mines; Stillwater is a mining town whose residents are like family. The town often prefers potlucks to single home meals, especially during times of celebration. The town is well known for its mines as they are home to various minerals, stones, and cave-dwelling creatures and plants. The townsfolk all harvest town square was made into a bazaar of sorts with the general store and mason in their own building around town.
