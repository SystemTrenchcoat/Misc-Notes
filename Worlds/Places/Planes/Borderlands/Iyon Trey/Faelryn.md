A wilderness ever-changing sitting between [[Aun Materia]] and [[Ceylium]]. Many guardians reside here, but tricksters and predators also call this place home. Here, magic and time are raw and wild, unpredictable and fickle with a hint of whimsy.

# [[Borderlands|Borderland]]

Otherwise known as the [[Faelryn|Realm of the Fae]], Faelryn is the [[Borderlands|borderland]] of [[Ceylium|The Heavens]]. [[Faelryn]] is where the spirits of nature come to life. Everything in [[Faelryn]] is alive with magic and consciousness. Full of the spirits of plants and animals alike, mirrors of mortals and many (mostly animal) [celestials](angels) meaning that theoretically, one could get to either plane from here. Good luck trying though, [the realm](Faelryn) is highly magical and responds very viscerally to thoughts and emotion, ever-changing and dizzying for an untrained mind to deal with. [[Fae]], from the spirits of nature to the magical mirror of the material beings to the everything in-between, are vastly different from one another, each with their own intentions and desires, but most, if not all, have a capacity for reason. Remember this traipsing through their home, it could mean the difference between being guided through the madness or being cursed to remain forever.
