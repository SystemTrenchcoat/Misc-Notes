A blinding white plane between [[The Void]] and [[Aun Materia]]. Here, void-walkers scream for freedom and psions grow their strength. Nothing exists as it would in [the Material](Aun%20Materia.md) here, all but the most extreme ideas of its inhabitants and visitors.

# [[Borderlands|Borderland]]

The [[Mynixaceli|Realm Between Sanity]], Mynixaceli is the borderland of [[The Void]]. [[The Void]] is the originator of all existence, pulling anything that gets close into its mono-tonal consciousness, the will of [[Chryorous]]. For those approaching from the material, this results in a pressure on their mind and being as their very existence tries to return to its originator. This pressure can be fought, but at the cost of everything the unfurnate creature was as their entire existence becomes the opposite of [[Chryorous]]'s will, with some of their own individuality of course. From the opposite end, the [[void-walkers]] get to have a form and self outside of [[Chryorous|The One Mind]], creating all manor of different creatures one from [[Aun Materia|The Material Plane]] could not dream up in their darkest nightmares. The mirrors of [[Aun Materia|The Material Plane]] come from thoughts and ideas manifesting into a physical form. Altrouism, tyranny, charity, greed, all turned into something to fit them perfectly. This is also the home of aberrations, creatures who were created through thought with no place on [[Aun Materia|The Material Plane]]. All of them are uncanny or grotesque to most [[Aun Materia|Material Realm]] inhabitants, though many of them come from the most intelligent creatures in the deepest seas, their thoughts powerful enough create avatars of themselves [[Mynixaceli|the in-between]], their evolutionary path veering wildly from mortals to allow this.
