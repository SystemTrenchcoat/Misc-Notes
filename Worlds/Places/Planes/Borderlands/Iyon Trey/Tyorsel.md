A nexus for the least physical energies to find physicality, this is the border realm of [[Infernis]]. Emotions and impulses are amplified until manifested, as an echo, a being created from a powerful feeling or experience. Benign or malignant, it's prime desire is to escape and be.

# [[Borderlands|Borderland]]

[[Tyorsel|Realm of the Shadow]] is a place of darkness, obsession, and decay. The darkness reigns supreme in the [[borderlands]] of [[Infernis|The Hells]]. The desolate wastes are spotted with fanatic lunatics, decaying creatures, and dark reflections of material creatures. Many [[demons]] walk through the desolate road before gaining entry to [[Aun Materia|The Material Plane]], fending off its deranged denizens like many mortals who walk through here. The creatures of the [[Tyorsel]] are widely natives, but wanderers can be corrupted and join their ranks. The [[Tyorsel]] is a challenging place devoid of any naturally occurring light illustrating quite clearly what happens to those who forsake the light for darkness. Terrifying power, but at what price... While [[Infernis|The Hells]] are filled with denizens who are fueled by emotion and desire, the [[Tyorsel]] is full of creatures who long for life in every possible way.
