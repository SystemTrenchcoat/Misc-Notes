[The different realms of existence](Iyon%20Trey) for centuries have been unable to interact with one another. They exist at different densities, different frequencies, with the denizens unable to bridge the gap. [[Aun Materia|The Material Plane]] changed all of this. Since it was created through energies of all 3 preceding planes, it allowed doorways to form into them. This started with thought, telepathic messages sent from extra-planars to [[mortals]] and [other intelligent material creatures](Before%20Mortals) on [[Aun Materia|The Material Plane]]. Over time, this evolved into portals, allowing [[angels]] and [[demons]] to walk among the mortals. Then, a new kind of life, and a new kind of plane. The [[Iyon Trey]], [[Ceylium|The Heavens]], [[Infernis|The Hells]], and [[The Void]], affected life on the material to enough of a degree that echoes were made to reflect this. These echoes, warped by the other planes' influence would give rise to the 3 [[Borderlands]] to reflect the affect of their presence.
