The original realm was the void. Nothingness and oneness echoing through the black. It eventually happened that the realm desired more than its monotony, and from this came the twins known as [[Xydranous]] and [[Sidraous]]. [[Sidraous]] encompassed the order, the desire of conformity and peace that was so present in [[The Void]]. [[Xydranous]], in contrast, was every aspect of individuality turned up to 11. Whereas [[Sidraous]] firmly kept to order and keeping all in peace, [[Xydranous]] was emotion and passion unbridled, he existed solely to spark desire and individuality no matter where he went. [[Chryorous]] was used to being the only presence in his realm, though the realm itself was, in a sense, conscious, a part of all, and knowing all. [[Chryorous]] did not like this new force affecting his echoes. With every passing moment, he could feel the chaos of [[Xydranous]] fighting with the order of [[Sidraous]], disturbing his peace with unrest. Thus, he gave them some void essence, a piece of his domain, which they could use to create their own realm like his. The two both gladly accepted and went off to create their individual paradises. These 3 planes are known through creation as the [[Iyon Trey]], the initial 3 realms of existence.
