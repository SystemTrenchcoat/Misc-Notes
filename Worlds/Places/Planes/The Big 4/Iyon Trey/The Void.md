A blanket of darkness across a realm of sheer consciousness. There are no indivisuals here, though they are widely echoes of the one known as [[Chryorous]]. They think through his thoughts, see through their perspective, but only as a hive. The fabric of the void also has thought. It decides how it wishes to shape itself, how to best assist the whole of the void, a co-creator of [[Chryorous]], though while [[Chryorous]] governs creatures, they govern lands. 

It is said that the void is formless, though this is not wholly true. The shapes change and twist ever on in the dark, everchanging to suit it. The people change from the presence of other energies interacting upon it, behaving in more contrast to [[Chryorous]], like a prism refracts a slightly different light. The land changes vastly with the creation of new planes. New shapes and forms create piecework of the other realms made using its power. [[Chryorous]] is said to dislike this, though the [[void-walkers]] don't mind.
