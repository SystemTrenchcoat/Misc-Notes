Endless wilderness encircling the [Material](Aun%20Materia) afterlife, here exists all flora and fauna at once. These environs are a druid's dream, though are more formidable than any biome in the [Material Plane](Aun%20Materia). It is, in a way, a magical menagerie of magnificent monsters.
