## Overview

McGucket coded artillerist of [[Scornhollow]]. A bandit with a knack for tinkering, he quickly became a dealer in an arms race against Epithesi Town. In death, he went to [[Scornhollow]] and had a number of run-ins with death. One eventful death, he ended up in [[The Void]] instead of [[Scornhollow]] for an entire eve. He was never the same after, hurling himself into his work as whispers ate away at his mind. Eventually, [[Skotadi]] found him, but by then, he was a shell of who he once was, but had exquisite weapons, as if forged by another mind. He requested the help of [[Skotadi]] to protect him and get him out of his purgatory. They were all too happy to help, inflicting a great toll on his already weakened mind.
