## The Legendary Sensei

Certified master of martial arts, Eris is respected across the entirety of [[Eotal]]. She is sought after by many aspiring warriors to hone their skills. Finding them is the first test as they move across the most remote and dangerous parts of [[Eotal]]. This makes identifying where to start tremendously difficult. However, those who find her powerful teacher. They have a strict regimen and code that she ingrains in her students, but following it is the key to her respect. That said, failing this in any way results in banishment
